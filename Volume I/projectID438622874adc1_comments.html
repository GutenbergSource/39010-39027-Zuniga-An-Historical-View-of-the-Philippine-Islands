About the Author

Joaquín Martínez de Zúñiga (1760-1818) was an Augustian Lecturer and Priest, who lived and worked in the Philippines for many years.

About the Work

This is one of earliest histories of the Philippines in the English language, publised in 1814 in London by J. Asperne, as a translation of "Historia de las Islas Philipinas" published near Manila at the Impreso en Sampaloc in 1803. The Spanish original is extremely rare.

This English translation was last republished in 1966 by the Filipiniana Book Guild in Manila.
